Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2024

1. Who We Are

M S Upholstery is a upholstery business based in Sydenham, Christchurch, New Zealand. This policy explains how we collect and use your personal information.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information when you submit an enquiry through our website, contact us by phone or email, or use our services. This may include your name, phone number, email address, and details of your enquiry.

3. How We Use It

  • To respond to your enquiry or quote request
  • To arrange and carry out work
  • To contact you about your job or order
  • To improve our service based on feedback

We do not sell or share your details with third parties except where required to perform our services or by law.

4. Forms and Third Parties

Our contact forms are processed through FormSubmit (formsubmit.co). Submissions are forwarded to our email. This website does not use tracking cookies or analytics that collect personal data.

5. Your Rights

Under the New Zealand Privacy Act 2020 you may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information. Contact us at kmsinclair@xtra.co.nz.
